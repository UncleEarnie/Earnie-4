The Visibility Index

Know how you make decisions when things get uncertain.

Earnie runs short interactive scenarios that simulate uncertainty and volatility, then turns your behaviour into a simple score and personalised insights.

Not financial advice. Not a personality test. Built to help you understand your decision patterns.

What is the Visibility Index?

The Visibility Index is a measure of decision clarity and steadiness under stress. Most tools ask what you think you would do. Earnie observes what you actually do when decisions feel real.
