Risk Level: Medium Risk

Balanced funds mix growth assets (shares, property) with income assets (cash, bonds). Good middle ground for most people.

When to Review

Review your KiwiSaver settings at least once a year, or when major life events occur such as changing jobs, getting married, or as you approach retirement.

Time Horizon Matters

If you are young with many years until retirement, you can typically afford to take more risk with growth funds. As you get closer to retirement, consider moving to more conservative options.
